Tansy Burns

Research Scientist

I am the research coordinator for Simonscmap, a collection of harmonized data and open-source tools developed to investigate the hidden worlds of ocean microbes. I coordinate work and planning within the Simons-CMAP group, and I am also the point of contact for collaborators and Simons-CMAP users. My career has spanned ocean research, program/project management, and education, and I most enjoy working where these areas intersect.
